Encryption — the cartoon version

Tier 3 + Tier 4 · Alice writes, Bob reads, Eve eavesdrops, signature proves who wrote it.

1
Setup
Both share the workspace passphrase. Each has their own signing key.
2
Alice writes
She types markdown into the editor.
3
Encrypt + sign
Compressed, encrypted with workspace key, signed with Alice's key. Wax seal "A".
4
In transit / at rest
Eve can see the file. She sees only ciphertext. No content, no edits possible undetected.
5
Bob opens it
Bob has the workspace passphrase. Argon2id derives the key, unwraps CEK, decrypts body, decompresses.
6
"From Alice ✓"
Alice's signing key public part is in the file. Bob verifies. Editor shows "by Alice ✓" badge.
